About CAIN in the Curriculum

The CAIN Archive is a collection of information and source material on 'the Troubles' and politics in Northern Ireland from 1968 to the present. This program attempts to encourage teachers in Northern Ireland and the Republic of Ireland as well as Leaving Certificate students to use the resources available. The resources have been organised by topics and subcategories in the respective curricula. Furthermore, additional resources from organisations outside of CAIN, such as RTE, BBC, UTV, Ulster Museum have been linked to help contextualise the events of 'The Troubles'.

Dr Matthew Milliken, Ulster University, undertook much of the work of mapping CAIN resources to the GCSE History curriculum in Northern Ireland, Junior Cycle History and Leaving Certificate History in the Republic of Ireland.

A forum is available to suggest resources that could be added and to discuss issues regarding the teaching of the topic.
